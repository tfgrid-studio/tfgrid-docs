# Contact

Get in touch with the TFGrid Studio team.

---

## Email Contacts

### General Inquiries

**[contact@tfgrid.studio](mailto:contact@tfgrid.studio)**

Questions, feedback, partnerships, or just want to say hello.

- General questions about TFGrid Studio
- Partnership opportunities
- Feedback and suggestions
- Media inquiries

**Response time:** 3-5 business days

---

### Technical Support

**[support@tfgrid.studio](mailto:support@tfgrid.studio)**

Need help with deployments, troubleshooting, or technical questions.

- Deployment issues
- Configuration help
- Bug reports (non-security)
- Technical questions

**Response time:** 2-3 business days

---

### Enterprise Sales

**[enterprise@tfgrid.studio](mailto:enterprise@tfgrid.studio)**

Custom deployments, SLA, dedicated support, or volume licensing.

- Custom deployment requirements
- Service Level Agreements (SLA)
- Dedicated support plans
- Volume licensing
- White-label solutions

**Response time:** 1-2 business days

---

### Security Reports

**[security@tfgrid.studio](mailto:security@tfgrid.studio)**

Report security vulnerabilities responsibly. We take security seriously.

- Security vulnerabilities
- Privacy concerns
- Responsible disclosure

**Response time:** Within 48 hours

For detailed security reporting guidelines, see our [Security Policy](security.md).
